Mr Cypher Samosa

  • one samosa
  • 30min-prep time
  • 10min-cook time
  • 40min-Total
  • 2 servings

    • INGREDIENTS

    • 1 cup all purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
    • 1/2kg of minced meat
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/4 cup water
    • 2 cup refined oil
    • salt as required
    • 1 teaspoon of red chilli

    PROCEDURE

    • boil the minced meat
    • add spices and boiled meat and cook for a while
    • prepare the dough for the samosa
    • roll the dough in small puris and cut into half
    • fill the semi-circle with meat filling and deep fry
